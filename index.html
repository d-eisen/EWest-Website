Empower West Louisville

About Us

Card subtitle

EmpowerWest is a coalition of Louisville area pastors and churches seeking to unleash the educational, economic, and spiritual power of West Louisville residents so that they might maximize their God-given potential and capacity.

Mission

Empower West will:

  1. Lift a prophetic voice that advocates with and amplifies the voices of those in West Louisville in places of power.
  2. Help change the hearts of people in and outside of West Louisville so that people will see the importance of West Louisville and her people in all parts of Greater Louisville.
  3. Find ways to increase economic and educational opportunity for residents of West Louisville.
  4. Build bridges of reconciliation between our churches and among our members.
  5. Continually educate ourselves and others as to how to bring our hearts to this in a long-term productive way.
